Our Solutions

Our Core Expertise

Enterprise-grade technologies tailored for high-volume document processing.

Intelligent OCR

Advanced Tesseract and OpenCV integration to digitize handwritten and typed "whitemail" with 99% accuracy, even at scales of 25,000 documents per day.

Vulnerability NLP

Proprietary Natural Language Processing models designed to detect critical risk markers like eviction notices or disability support needs in real-time.

Privacy-by-Design

Local AES-256 and Fernet encryption ensures that Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is secured before it ever reaches the server.

Serverless Scaling

Architected for the cloud using Python and FastAPI to ensure 99.9% uptime and zero-latency during peak ingestion periods.
